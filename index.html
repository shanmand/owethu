About Prof Khoza-Shangase

Professor Katijah Khoza-Shangase graduated from the University of the Witwatersrand where she currently still lectures. She obtained her PhD as the first Black graduate to be awarded a PhD from a South African university in her field.

She has extensive clinical experience and is also an active researcher. Her multilinguism allows her to offer efficient services to patients from diverse languages and cultures.
